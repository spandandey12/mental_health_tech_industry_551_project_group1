## 1. Team Goals and Values
Our primary goal is to collaboratively design and implement a high-quality, interactive data dashboard that meets the requirements of DATA 551 while providing meaningful insights to a defined target audience.  
We value respectful communication, transparency, accountability, and mutual support throughout the project.

---

## 2. Work Distribution
- Tasks will be divided based on individual strengths and availability, including (but not limited to):
  - Data selection and exploratory analysis
  - Dashboard design and visualization
  - Application development and deployment
  - Documentation and writing
- Responsibilities will be discussed and agreed upon before each milestone.
- If a team member is unable to complete a task on time, they are expected to notify the group as early as possible so responsibilities can be adjusted.

---

## 3. Communication
- Primary communication will take place via:
  - GitHub Issues and Pull Requests (for technical tasks)
  - Messaging platforms (e.g., Slack/Discord) for coordination
- Team members are expected to respond to messages within a reasonable time frame (usually within 24 hours on weekdays).

---

## 4. Meetings
- Regular check-ins will occur as needed, especially before milestone deadlines.
- Meetings may be held online or in person depending on availability.
- Agendas will be agreed upon in advance, and brief notes or action items will be shared afterward.

---

## 5. GitHub Workflow
- All work will be tracked through GitHub.
- Team members should pull the latest changes before starting work.
- For major changes, a pull request will be created and reviewed by at least one other team member before merging.
- Smaller changes may be committed directly to the main branch with team awareness.

---

## 6. Quality Expectations
- Each team member is responsible for producing clear, well-documented, and reproducible work.
- Code, visualizations, and written materials should follow best practices taught in the MDS program.
- Constructive feedback is encouraged and should be given respectfully.

---

## 7. Availability
- Team members will communicate any periods of limited availability (e.g., travel, exams, personal commitments) in advance.
- Flexibility and understanding are expected when scheduling work around these constraints.

---

## 8. Conflict Resolution
- Any disagreements or issues will first be discussed openly within the team.
- If a resolution cannot be reached, the team will seek guidance from the course TA or instructor.
- All members commit to addressing conflicts professionally and constructively.

---

## 9. Agreement
By participating in this project, all team members agree to adhere to the principles and expectations outlined in this contract.  
This document may only be updated with the agreement of all team members.
